Maintenance and inspection

I offer inspection for all types of equipment. The goal of inspection is to confirm proper operation and saftey.

Equipment inspections are $100 per piece of equipment.

Maintenace requirements vary by equipment type. Most types of equipment require little maintenance beyond filter changes. Some equipment, such as high efficiency boilers or fuel oil heaters, require careful annual cleaning and adjustment. Its best to call or text me at (406) 544-9555 to discuss your particular situation.

For true equipment maintenance, the cost depends on the type of equipment and what the equipment needs. Typical pricing is between $100 and $300 per piece of equipment.

Costs

My services are priced by referencing an industry standardized price book. However, I know it can be helpful to have a rough idea of what to expect.

In general, most repairs cost between $200 and $500, plus a $150 diagnostic fee. The actual cost depends on the equipment, the problem, and the parts or time required.

I will never perform services without clear communication about what is going to be done and what the costs will be. Agreement about services and costs is a priority.
