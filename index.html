Maintenance and inspection

Some equipment needs regular maintenance. Some only needs occasional attention. Either way, an inspection can be a good way to check safety, operation, and signs of trouble.

Equipment inspections are $100 per piece of equipment. For true maintenance work, the cost depends on the type of equipment and what the equipment needs.

Costs

I charge fair market prices using an industry standardized price book. I also know it is helpful to have a general idea of what to expect.

In general, most repairs cost between $200 and $500, plus a $150 diagnostic fee. The actual cost depends on the equipment, the problem, and the parts or time required.

I will never perform services without clear communication about what is going to be done and what the costs will be. Agreement about services and costs is a priority for me.
